1 Generate the maze:
	1 Pick a starting cell:
		1 Mark it visited
		2 Mark it as the SPAWN
	2 If any neighbouring cell hasnt been visited:
		1 Pick a random cell that hasnt been visited
		2 Remove the wall inbetween the two cells
		3 Add the current cell to the stack
		4 Make the chosen cell the current cell and mark it visited
		5 Condition MakeSpecial = true
	3 If the current cell has no unvisited neigbours:
		1 If Condition MakeSpecial == true:
			1 Mark current cell as SPECIAL
			2 Condition MakeSpecial = false
		2 Take the top cell from the stack and make it current
	4 Repeat from #2 until there are no cells left
	5 Mark the final cell as EXIT

2 Handle the logic of SPECIAL cells:
	1 If there are no SPECIAL type cells, clear the maze and start at 1
	2 Choose a random cell from the group, it becomes a BOSS mark
	3 While there are SPECIAL cells left pick a random cell and choose one at random:
			1 Make it an ENEMY cell
			2 Make it a LOOT cell
			
3 Send out a signal out to Enemy Handler to prepare enemy logic
